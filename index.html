About Me

Who I Am

I'm a Computer Science graduate and Lecturer with a solid foundation in programming, data analysis, and machine learning. Currently teaching at Sylhet Agricultural University, I believe in learning by doing — every lesson I design is built around practical, real-world applications.

What drives me is the challenge of making hard ideas feel obvious. Whether it's breaking down a neural network or leading a hands-on Python workshop across multiple universities, I strive to build environments where curiosity is celebrated and confidence grows.

Outside the classroom, I love travelling, playing sports, and meeting people from all walks of life — because the best ideas come from the most unexpected conversations.
